Shelter to Shelter

A tool for hikers on the Appalachian Trail

Hiking on the trail may give you the oppritunty to think about life, the universe and everything, but no question is more important than "where are you going to sleep tonight?". With this app, you can find out which shelters will mark a good stopping point after a long day's hike.
